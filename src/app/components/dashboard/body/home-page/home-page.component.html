A Warm Welcome!

The Aeon’s Study Circle was founded in June 2017. The Aeon’s Study Circle is meant to advance the career prospects of young people who have the potential for high –ranking Civil Service & Government Services placement but can’t afford the expensive coaching available only in big Cities. As it is, quite a lot of our young people who are talented and would compete with skill and tenacity are handicapped by being unable to get access to quality coaching and up-to-date study materials. Some candidate opts out of the race for want of proper motivation or through the mistaken notion that for a person with non-urban background Civil Service placement is an impossible dream. The Aeon’s Study Circle group expects to bring Civil Service, Kerala Administrative Service, State Psc exams, SET& NET training within easy reach of the candidates and it hopes to fulfill this mission by providing top quality training at the free of cost.This study group will be online only.It can be found on all social media.
